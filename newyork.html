Travel in New York

Manhattan and Brooklyn are separated by a river. The Brooklyn Bridge is the most symbolic bridge on the Hudson River. "Scent of a Woman", "Spider-Man","The Godfather", etc. They all seem to be different business cards in different New York's movies. Just like the Golden Gate Bridge in San Francisco, The Brooklyn Bridge is the most spectacular connection to New York.

The Statue of Liberty is a gift from the people of France to the people of the United States, was designed by French sculptor Frédéric Auguste Bartholdi. The statue became an icon of freedom and of the United States. It is a welcoming sight to immigrants arriving from abroad.
